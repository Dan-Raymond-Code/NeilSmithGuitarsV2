Stardust 24



The Stardust is a modern instrument with classic sensibilities intact. Built with the professional musician in mind. Features include among others things stainless steel frets and an expanded upper register without sacrificing tone, resonance or sustain often only possible in 22 fret designs. Deep contours and nice balance - the Stardust is a player axe.
