Stardust II



Not your grandpa's guitar. The Stardust is a modern instrument combining classic designs with exquisite tone and playability. It features comfortable arm and belly contours, effortless access to the 24th fret, and compelling ergonomics. The Stardust is a brighter decidedly "in your face" guitar that has its own voice, with dynamics, output and presence perfect for rock and fusion.
