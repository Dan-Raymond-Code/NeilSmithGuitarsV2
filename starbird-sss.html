Starbird SSS



Not your grandpa's guitar. The Starbird is a modern instrument combining classic designs with exquisite tone and playability. It features comfortable arm and belly contours, effortless access to the 22nd fret, and compelling ergonomics. The Starbird has its own voice, with expanded sustain, resonance, and tonal options.
