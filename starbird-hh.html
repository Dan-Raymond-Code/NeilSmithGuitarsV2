Starbird HH



A lightweight guitar with a big sound. The Starbird HH is a modern instrument combining classic designs, light weight, and exquisite tone and playability. It features comfortable arm and belly contours, effortless access to the highest fret, and addictive ergonomics. The Starbird HH has its own voice, with expanded sustain, resonance, and tone-shaping options.
